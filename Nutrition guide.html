Dear friends, today we will bring you some constructive suggestions to better control your nutritional intake in daily life and achieve a healthy diet. Let's take a closer look!

  • 1. Eat a varied diet with a balanced intake of various nutrients

    In order for the body to be adequately nourished, we should be varied in our daily diet. This can be achieved in the following ways: Try vegetables and fruits of various colors, each containing different vitamins and minerals. Every meal should have the right amount of high-quality protein, such as fish, chicken, tofu, etc. Choose healthy sources of fat, such as olive oil, avocados, and nuts.

    2. Appropriately control the distribution of plates, following the principle of "half a plate of vegetables, a quarter of a plate of carbohydrates, and a quarter of a plate of protein"

    This plate distribution ensures that you get enough nutrients at each meal. At the same time, it also helps to control food intake and avoid excessive intake.

    3. Pay attention to the rhythm of eating, chew slowly and swallow

    Develop good eating habits that aid digestion and absorption. Chewing slowly allows you to taste food better and helps reduce hunger so you don't overeat。
